Trees

Trees are the green cover of the planet.
Trees need water, sunshine and air to grow.
The process of photosynthesis that occurs in trees, which helps them grow, uses oxygen and gives out carbon dioxide.
Thus, they contribute to the oxygen in the air that we need to breathe to live.
They also use up the carbon dioxide that is present in the air and thus prevent the accumulation of greenhouse gas that adds to global warming and climate change.
